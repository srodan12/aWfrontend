Thanks for your interest. It was our duty to help James in their final moments and recreate what could have been the most iconic zine of 90's.

The original UtopiaZine was found in a computer inside the attic of James Buchanan. A revered curator of arbor.org. In his final days, he claimed to have visited a clandestine government facility that experimented with time travel. As James's condition got worse, he began to tell about SRodan and his friends, creating art and posting them in a globally surrounding computer called Ethereum. We searched the Registry of Names and found none of the people referred in this document exist. This is a test to see if the document will actually be created in the future. Apparently, a user called deus worked on realizing that dream according to the notes left.

"two people that never knew eachother walk away.
worlds converging and diverging. sinusoids upon sinusoids.
summation and fourier. halls of a complex unknown.
within the next 2 steps, they knew everything about their lives prior.

another watches, bloodshot eyes. moons dividing moons. headed now.
clear skies and rivers flooding the dormant salmon. downstream data traffic."

-James Buchanan
